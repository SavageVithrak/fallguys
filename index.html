About Fall Race

Description

Fall Race is a battle royale game developed by Mediatonic in 2020. Pass through unruly competitors and the inflexible laws of physics to become the last player.

Up to 60 players can compete against each other in a range of obstacles, such as roads or day days, control jelly bean-like characters.

The characters are shown as colorful beans. In a short sweep from round to escalating mess, candidates will be clustered into an online community before a winner is left! Your task is to combat all the obstacles and resolve them. Try to defeat the rival in the next round to beat him the highest.

Use all your skills like to escape, leap, climb ladders, run quickly, and much more. Try to defeat the rival in the next round to beat him the highest. Before the time expires, you must try to get to the finish. You're going to be disqualified otherwise.

Feature of Fall Race

  • Massive Online Pandemonium: Game up with ridiculous challenges and wild obstacle courses, all players with the hopes of making the cut and advancing to the next round of mayhem.

  • Competitive & Cooperative: Shift between competitive free-for-alls and cooperative challenges where the losing team all get eliminated!

  • Comically Physical: See your fellow competitors bend, bounce, and bash their way to hilarious.

  • Delightfully Customizable: You can customize characters in Fall Guys: from fashionable pineapple couture to the latest in bunny hats.

Development & Release date

Fall Race was developed by HiHoy and released in August 2020.

How to controls

  • Arrow keys or "W,A,S,D" to move
  • Space to jump.
